Jellyfin

The Free Software Media System

Jellyfin is a self-hosted media server solution. Similar to Plex or Emby, except that Jellyfin is fully open source software and free with no strings attached. It is developed by a team of volunteers and nobody makes money from the project.

I joined the Jellyfin development team after making a few initial PRs improving the Live TV section of the Android TV app. I have been as active as possible in with the project since reviewing PRs and helping fix bugs and implement new features.
